Instructions for Authors

Submission Guidelines & Publication Ethics

Manuscript Preparation

  • Manuscript must be original and unpublished.
  • Maximum 200 pages allowed.
  • Plagiarism should be below 10%.
  • Follow APA referencing style.
  • Submit paper in MS Word format.

Review Process

All submitted manuscripts undergo a double-blind peer review process. Authors will be notified regarding acceptance, revision, or rejection after editorial evaluation.

Ethical Policy

Authors must ensure originality of work, proper citation, and adherence to academic integrity standards. Any form of plagiarism or data fabrication will lead to rejection.
