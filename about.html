About the Journal

IBM – International Journal of Business Management is a peer-reviewed, refereed, open-access international journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research in business and management sciences.

The journal provides a global platform for academicians, researchers, industry professionals, and scholars to share innovative research, theoretical insights, and practical developments.

Aims & Objectives

• Promote excellence in research and academic publishing.
• Encourage interdisciplinary management research.
• Provide a forum for global academic discussion.
• Support innovation in business and management studies.
• Bridge academia and industry practices.

Scope of the Journal

The journal publishes research articles, review papers, case studies, and conceptual papers in Strategic Management, Financial Management, Marketing, Human Resource Management, Organizational Behavior, Operations & Supply Chain Management, Entrepreneurship, Innovation Management, E-Commerce, Retail Management, Tourism Management, Project Management, Risk & Insurance and related management disciplines.
